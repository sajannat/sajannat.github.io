Elias Hossain

I'm studying my PhD in Computer Science & Engineering at Mississippi State University, United States. My research focuses on using artificial intelligence in healthcare. With 18 papers published in prestigious journals and conferences, my research centers on natural language processing for electronic health records and machine learning applications. In my three years of industry experience, I served as a Software Engineer for a year and spent two years as a machine learning researcher. Within biomedicine, I've made significant contributions by applying deep learning algorithms to detect diseases like cancer from extensive biomedical imaging data, including chest X-rays, MRI and CT scans. Additionally, I've explored the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) for clinical text classification, employing techniques like fine-tuning and zero-shot learning. The outcomes of my research have been published in reputable journals such as Springer, Elsevier, ACM, and PubMed. I look forward to contributing to future advancements in the field.
