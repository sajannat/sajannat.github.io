Research

  • Fake News Detection: Developing algorithms for identifying and categorizing fake news using natural language processing (NLP) techniques. The research aims to enhance the accuracy and reliability of automated fake news detection systems.
  • Brain Cancer Detection: Using deep learning algorithms to analyze MRI scans for early detection and classification of brain cancer. The objective is to contribute to more effective and timely diagnosis of brain cancer cases.
  • Mental Health Research: Investigating the application of artificial intelligence in mental health, including sentiment analysis of text data and machine learning models to predict mental health conditions. The research aims to provide valuable insights and support for mental health professionals.
  • Diabetes Prediction: Developing intelligent prediction models using machine learning, specifically Light Gradient Boosting Machine (LightGBM), for the early diagnosis of diabetes. The study explores the integration of smartphone technology for convenient and accessible diabetes prediction.
  • Kidney Disease Classification: Applying machine learning techniques to classify and diagnose kidney diseases based on medical data and diagnostic indicators. The research aims to contribute to the development of accurate and efficient systems for kidney disease classification.
  • Bone Fracture Identification: Investigating the use of deep learning algorithms for the identification and classification of bone fractures in medical imaging data such as X-rays. The focus is on enhancing the speed and accuracy of fracture detection in clinical settings.
  • NLP in Electronic Health Records (EHRs): Exploring the application of natural language processing techniques in analyzing electronic health records. The research involves developing models that can extract valuable information from unstructured clinical text, contributing to more efficient healthcare data analysis and decision-making.
  • Mobile Application Development for Health Monitoring: Researching and developing mobile applications for health monitoring, incorporating features such as real-time data collection, analysis, and user-friendly interfaces. The project aims to enhance accessibility to healthcare information and promote proactive health management through mobile technology.
  • Blind Assistant and Safe Navigation: Designing and implementing a comprehensive blind assistance and safe navigation system for both indoor and outdoor environments. The research focuses on developing technologies to improve the independence and safety of individuals with visual impairments.
  • Safety System Mobile Apps for Kidnapping Prevention: Developing mobile applications with safety features aimed at preventing kidnapping incidents. The research involves the integration of real-time tracking, emergency alerts, and proactive measures to enhance personal safety through mobile technology.
